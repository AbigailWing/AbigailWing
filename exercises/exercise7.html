Writing Samples

Microsoft Word 2019 for Writing Tutors Manual (PDF)
I wrote this 26-page manual on Microsoft Word 2019 for Writing Tutors as a semester-long project for a technical writing class. I created this manual for tutors to use when collaborating with tutees in the UMass Amherst writing center. It covers topics such as sharing a Word document, tracking changes, fixing spelling and grammar errors, and inserting citations.

Fresco y Sano: Reducing Food Insecurity in Lawrence, MA Grant Proposal (PDF)
I created this 22-page grant proposal on establishing a plant-based mobile food pantry in Lawrence, MA as a semester-long project for a professional writing class. This grant proposal sought $131,000 from the Food Security Infrastruction Grant Program (FSIG) to fund general infrastructure costs as well as staff wages.

Representations of Climate Refugees Final Paper (PDF)
I collaborated with my group members to produce an 8-page paper on representations of climate refugees for a junior year writing English class. This paper explores how neoliberal language prevents audiences from empathetically engaging with the issue of climate refugees.

The First Amendment and Hate Speech Final Paper (PDF)
I wrote this 11-page paper on the First Amendment and hate speech for a junior year writing history class. This paper explores different thinkers' views concerning free speech and hate speech to come to a better understanding of how to contend with hate speech in a modern context.
