I wrote this 26-page manual on Microsoft Word 2019 for Writing Tutors as a semester-long project for a technical writing class. I created this manual for tutors to use when collaborating with tutees in the UMass Amherst writing center. It covers topics such as sharing a Word document, tracking changes, fixing spelling and grammar errors, and inserting citations.
