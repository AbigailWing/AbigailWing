GUI Highlight Videos

Problem: Users needed quick, engaging resources to learn GUI functionalities efficiently.

Solution: Created a series of video tutorials highlighting core features, which involved the following steps:

  • Script Writing: I wrote detailed scripts in Microsoft Word to outline the content and flow of each tutorial. These scripts were designed to highlight core features, ensuring clarity and conciseness.
  • Narration: Once the scripts were finalized, a team member recorded the narration to guide users through the video.
  • Storyboarding: I created storyboards to visualize the sequence of the video, ensuring that each scene aligned with the script and effectively demonstrated GUI features.
  • Stakeholder Feedback: After drafting the videos, I shared them with stakeholders for feedback, making revisions as needed to meet user expectations and ensure the video accurately addressed their needs.
  • Editing: Using TechSmith Camtasia, I edited the videos by adding graphics, transitions, and visual cues to enhance the learning experience. For audio editing, I used Adobe Audiate to ensure clear and professional sound quality. Additionally, I incorporated captions to improve accessibility for all users.
  • Publishing: Finally, the updated videos were posted to YouTube, making them easily accessible to users.

Outcome: Increased user engagement and reduced time spent on support inquiries.
