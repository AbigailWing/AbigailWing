TracNet Coastal Documentation/Design Project

Problem: The TracNet Coastal and Coastal Pro Wi-Fi and 5G antenna systems required clear, user-friendly documentation to guide installation, setup, and maintenance. Frequent product updates during development added complexity, and the project needed to be completed on a tight timeline without compromising quality.

Solution: After being with the company for only a few months, I was entrusted with the role of lead technical writer for this project. I independently led the documentation process from start to finish, creating a full suite of deliverables, including installation guides, quick start guides, online Help, service forms (using JavaScript in Adobe Acrobat), field replaceable unit (FRU) instructions, reshipping instructions, and labeling instructions. I used Adobe FrameMaker, Photoshop, and Illustrator to design the installation guides, FRU instructions, reshipping instructions, and labeling instructions, and developed quick start guides in InDesign, and built online Help using RoboHelp. Throughout the project, I managed revisions and reviews in Adobe Workfront and collaborated closely with SMEs to ensure the content was accurate, clear, and effective for end users.

Outcome: I completed all documentation well in advance of the product launch, playing a key role in the seamless rollout of the TracNet Coastal systems. The high-quality, user-focused materials empowered end users and showcased my expertise in managing complex documentation projects, adapting to changing product needs, and collaborating effectively with cross-functional teams.
