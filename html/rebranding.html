V7-HTS to FM60-VSAT Rebranding Project

Problem: The TracPhone V7-HTS was being rebranded to the FM60-VSAT for third-party sale, which required the creation of new product documentation. This included an installation guide, user guide, quick start guide, and service provider addendum. The project had a tight deadline, and numerous product changes occurred throughout the process, requiring frequent updates to the materials.

Solution: To meet the deadline and adapt to the ongoing product changes, I created comprehensive and updated documentation using various tools:

  • Adobe FrameMaker for the installation and user guides, ensuring technical accuracy and professional formatting.
  • Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator for creating images to support the guides, offering clear, visual explanations.
  • The Quick Start Guide was designed using Adobe InDesign to provide users with an easy-to-understand introduction to the FM60-VSAT.
  • Additionally, I developed a Service Provider Addendum to address specific service-related instructions for providers.

Outcome: Despite the fast-paced nature of the project and the frequent product revisions, the documentation was successfully delivered on time. The new manuals were clear, user-friendly, and aligned with the FM60-VSAT rebranding, ensuring that third-party resellers, service providers, and end-users had the necessary resources to effectively use the product.
